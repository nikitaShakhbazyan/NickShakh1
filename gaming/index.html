After the death of the emperor, the empire was divided into four kingdoms, each of which was headed by the head of one of the strongest guilds, since then a tradition and an indisputable rule have arisen: Only the strongest will get power, and the strongest guild will have to carry the banner of their kingdom! "GAME NAME", this is a world full of battles, victories and defeats, courage of the warriors of the Empire!

According to legend....

It was predicted that in the lands of the four kingdoms, torn apart by internecine wars, the chosen one would come, who would unite the warring rulers and cleanse the world from the creatures of darkness.

A great warrior

You can go to the service of the emperor and get military glory and wealth, saving civilians from monster raids, or take a risk, overcome all difficulties and stand at the head of the state yourself, bringing prosperity and prosperity to your subjects. Whatever path you choose, friends will help you achieve your goal - thousands of players are ready to take your side, and a huge colorful world will make your adventures unforgettable.

Ling Feng

Ling Feng - the city of Ice Wind, was founded to defend the western borders of the Empire from demons. In times of turmoil, the Lingfeng seceded and elected their own emperor.

Pan Di

Pan Di - After the turmoil, the Commander-in-Chief ordered Minister Taishu Zhang to gather the heroes of the Celestial Empire and build Pan Di, the city of the Earthly Firmament. Since then, the blood of famous heroes has flowed in the veins of the Pandians.

Yan Tian

Yan Tian - the city of Fiery Heavens, separated from the other three kingdoms, was founded in the south of the empire by the famous warrior Feng Xi. The people of Yantian, honoring the founder, strive for military prowess and glory.

Cang Yun

Cang Yun - After the rebellion, the emperor rebuilt Cang Yun, the Blue Cloud City, where many heroes came. The Cangyun people occupied the east of the country, which was provided with everything necessary.
